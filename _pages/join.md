### About Hydrology and Water Resources Management Lab 
The **Hydrology and Water Resources Management Lab** studies environmental hydrology and water resources management with emphasis on environmental flows, sustainable allocation, and climate adaptation. We combine physics-based models (e.g., WEAP, HEC-ResSim), GIS/remote sensing, and data-driven methods (ML/DL) to understand change and support decision-making.
